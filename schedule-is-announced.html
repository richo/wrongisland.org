Pricing

As a few have been quick to point out, I stupidly managed to put the tickets up in AUD instead of NZD like you would expect. As a result you've all been charged somewhere in the ballpark of $53-$56 depending on what the exchange rate was doing at the time.

We're really sorry about that! It was an honest mistake, but the only way to fix it directly was to refund every ticket and then hassle people into rebuying them after changing it.

As a token of gratitude, I've thrown $5 * $badges_sold on top of what was already going on the tab.
